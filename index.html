The MATLAB Learning Center from Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education, Krishnankoil, in association with MathWorks and ARK, presents the CODY CHALLENGE – 24 Hours Hackathon as part of EUPHORIA 2026. Join students across disciplines to solve real‑world problems and showcase your expertise.

How It Works

  1. 1 Register using the link and pick your theme.
  2. 2 Qualify via MATLAB CODY Challenge.
  3. 3 Receive your problem statement and build.
  4. 4 Demo to judges and compete for prizes.

Hackathon Stages

  1. Stage 1 CODY Challenge — Solve MATLAB Cody problems to qualify.
  2. Stage 2 Problem Statement Assignment — Get a domain challenge in your chosen theme.
  3. Stage 3 Assessment — Build, submit, and face technical evaluation.
  4. Stage 4 Winner Selection — Present to judges; top teams awarded.
